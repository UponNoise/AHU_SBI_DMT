# HM10: Simple Linear Regression (简单线性回归)

## Part 1: Linear Regression Calculations (回归计算)

**Given Data (Q1-Q6):**
A company wants to analyze the relationship between the number of advertisements run per week ($x$) and the weekly sales revenue ($y$) in thousands of dollars.
(一家公司想要分析每周播放的广告数量 $x$ 与每周销售收入 $y$（千美元）之间的关系。)

| Week | Advertisements ($x$) | Sales Revenue ($y$) |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 1 | 1 | 12 |
| 2 | 2 | 18 |
| 3 | 3 | 24 |
| 4 | 4 | 30 |
| 5 | 5 | 34 |
| 6 | 6 | 38 |
| 7 | 7 | 41 |
| 8 | 8 | 45 |

**Intermediate Calculations (中间计算数据):**
*   $n = 8$
*   $\bar{x} = 4.5, \quad \bar{y} = 30.25$
*   $SS_{xx} = \sum(x-\bar{x})^2 = 42$
*   $SS_{yy} = SST = \sum(y-\bar{y})^2 = 929.5$
*   $SS_{xy} = \sum(x-\bar{x})(y-\bar{y}) = 196$

---

### Question 1
**Topic:** Intercept ($b_0$) Calculation
**题目主题：** 计算截距 ($b_0$)

**Question:**
Find the value of the intercept b0. (Answer in two decimal points)
找出截距 b0 的值。（保留两位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`9.25`

> **Note (解析):**
> 1.  先算斜率 $b_1 = \frac{SS_{xy}}{SS_{xx}} = \frac{196}{42} \approx 4.6667$
> 2.  再算截距 $b_0 = \bar{y} - b_1\bar{x} = 30.25 - (4.6667 \times 4.5) = 30.25 - 21.00 = 9.25$

---

### Question 2
**Topic:** Slope Coefficient ($b_1$) Calculation
**题目主题：** 计算斜率系数 ($b_1$)

**Question:**
Calculate the value of the slope coefficient b1. (Answer in two decimal points)
计算斜率系数 b1 的值。（保留两位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`4.67`

> **Note (解析):**
> $b_1 = \frac{SS_{xy}}{SS_{xx}} = \frac{196}{42} \approx 4.6667 \rightarrow 4.67$

---

### Question 3
**Topic:** Total Sum of Squares (SST)
**题目主题：** 总平方和 (SST)

**Question:**
Calculate SST (Answer in one decimal point)
计算 SST（保留一位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`929.5`

> **Note (解析):**
> $SST = \sum (y - \bar{y})^2 = SS_{yy} = 929.5$

---

### Question 4
**Topic:** Sum of Squares Regression (SSR)
**题目主题：** 回归平方和 (SSR)

**Question:**
Calculate SSR (Answer in one decimal point)
计算 SSR（保留一位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`914.7`

> **Note (解析):**
> $SSR = \frac{(SS_{xy})^2}{SS_{xx}} = \frac{196^2}{42} = \frac{38416}{42} \approx 914.67 \rightarrow 914.7$
> 或者 $SSR = b_1 \times SS_{xy} \approx 4.6667 \times 196 \approx 914.7$

---

### Question 5
**Topic:** Sum of Squares Error (SSE)
**题目主题：** 残差平方和 (SSE)

**Question:**
Calculate SSE (Answer in one decimal point)
计算 SSE（保留一位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`14.8`

> **Note (解析):**
> $SSE = SST - SSR = 929.5 - 914.7 = 14.8$

---

### Question 6
**Topic:** Coefficient of Determination ($r^2$)
**题目主题：** 判定系数 ($r^2$)

**Question:**
Find the coefficient of determination r^2. (Answer in 2 decimal points)
找出判定系数 r^2。（保留两位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`0.98`

> **Note (解析):**
> $r^2 = \frac{SSR}{SST} = \frac{914.7}{929.5} \approx 0.984 \rightarrow 0.98$

## Part 2: Hypothesis Testing & Analysis (假设检验与分析)

### Question 7
**Topic:** Hypothesis Test for Slope ($t$-test)
**题目主题：** 斜率的假设检验 ($t$ 检验)

**Question:**
Given $b = 0.3$, $s_b = 0.138$, and $n = 50$, use a $t$ test to do a hypothesis test on $H_0: B=0$ versus $H_a: B \neq 0$ at $\alpha = 0.05$.
给定样本斜率 $b=0.3$，斜率标准误 $s_b=0.138$，样本量 $n=50$。在 $\alpha=0.05$ 水平下进行双尾t检验。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
**Reject H_0** (拒绝原假设)

> **Note (解析):**
> 1.  计算检验统计量: $t = \frac{b - 0}{s_b} = \frac{0.3}{0.138} \approx 2.17$
> 2.  确定临界值: 自由度 $df = n - 2 = 48$。在 $\alpha=0.05$ 下，双尾临界值 $t_{crit} \approx 2.01$。
> 3.  结论: $|2.17| > 2.01$，落入拒绝域，因此拒绝 $H_0$。

### Question 8
**Topic:** Calculating $r^2$ from Sums of Squares
**题目主题：** 根据平方和计算 $r^2$

**Question:**
Given $\sum(y-\bar{y})^2 = 122.5$ and $\sum e^2 = 43.2$, find $r^2$. (State your answer in two decimal points)
给定总平方和($SST$)=122.5 和 残差平方和($SSE$)=43.2，求 $r^2$。（保留两位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`0.65`

> **Note (解析):**
> $r^2 = 1 - \frac{SSE}{SST} = 1 - \frac{43.2}{122.5} = 1 - 0.3526... \approx 0.647 \rightarrow 0.65$

### Question 9
**Topic:** Estimating Regression Parameters from Graph
**题目主题：** 从图表估计回归参数

**Question:**
Given the following graph of $y = a + bx$, estimate $b$ and $a$.
根据给出的直线图，估计截距 $a$ 和斜率 $b$。
*(Graph shows a line passing through y-intercept at 3 and x-intercept at 5)*

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
**$a=3, b=-3/5$**

> **Note (解析):**
> *   **截距 ($a$):** 直线与Y轴交点为正数，看起来是 3。
> *   **斜率 ($b$):** 直线向下倾斜，斜率为负。从 (0, 3) 到 (5, 0)，$\text{rise} = -3, \text{run} = 5$，所以 $b = -3/5$。

### Question 10
**Topic:** Point Estimate Prediction
**题目主题：** 点估计预测

**Question:**
Given the regression line $\hat{y} = 1.25x + 4.5$, find a point estimate for $y$ when $x = 1.5$. (State your answer in two decimal points)
给定回归方程 $\hat{y} = 1.25x + 4.5$，当 $x=1.5$ 时求 $y$ 的点估计值。（保留两位小数）。

**Correct Answer (正确答案):**
`6.38`

> **Note (解析):**
> $\hat{y} = 1.25(1.5) + 4.5 = 1.875 + 4.5 = 6.375$
> Round to two decimal points: $6.38$
