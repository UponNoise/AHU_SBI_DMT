## 1. Picking a card from a standard deck of playing cards, what is the probability of getting a red card which is a non-ace card? (Give your answer to 3 decimal places)
**从一副标准扑克牌中抽牌，抽到红色且非A的牌的概率是多少？（答案保留3位小数）**

**Answer:** 0.462

**Steps:**
- Total cards = 52
- Red cards = 26 (hearts and diamonds)
- Red aces = 2
- Red non-ace cards = 26 - 2 = 24
- Probability = 24 ÷ 52 ≈ 0.4615 ≈ 0.462

**知识点:** 古典概率计算 (Classical Probability)

---

## 2. How many possible outcomes are there when both a coin and a four sided die are tossed? (Answer as an integer)
**同时抛一枚硬币和一个四面骰子，有多少种可能的结果？（答案为整数）**

**Answer:** 8

**Steps:**
- Coin outcomes = 2 (Head, Tail)
- Four-sided die outcomes = 4
- Total outcomes = 2 × 4 = 8

**知识点:** 乘法原理 (Multiplication Principle)

## 3. If P(B')=0.7 and P(A|B) =0.1, find P(A and B). Give your answer to 2 decimal places
**如果P(B')=0.7且P(A|B)=0.1，求P(A and B)。答案保留2位小数**

**Answer:** 0.03

**Steps:**
- P(B′) = 0.7 ⇒ P(B) = 1 - 0.7 = 0.3
- P(A|B) = P(A and B) ÷ P(B) ⇒ 0.1 = P(A and B) ÷ 0.3
- P(A and B) = 0.1 × 0.3 = 0.03

**知识点:** 条件概率与补事件概率 (Conditional Probability & Complement)

---

## 4. Determine if the following events involving the throw of a six-sided die are independent : A= getting a multiple of 2, B= getting a 4 or 6
**判断在投掷六面骰子时以下事件是否独立：A=得到2的倍数，B=得到4或6**

**Answer:** No, events A and B are not independent

**Steps:**
- P(A) = 3/6 = 1/2 (numbers 2, 4, 6)
- P(B) = 2/6 = 1/3 (numbers 4, 6)
- P(A and B) = 2/6 = 1/3 (numbers 4, 6)
- Check: P(A) × P(B) = (1/2) × (1/3) = 1/6 ≠ 1/3
- Therefore, not independent

**知识点:** 事件独立性判断 (Independence of Events)

**考虑连续不放回抽取两张牌的实验，抽到人头牌视为成功。已知A=第一次抽到人头牌，求B=第二次抽到人头牌的概率。答案以分数形式表示**

## 6. Find the probability of at least one head in four tosses of a fair coin (heads and tails have the same chance of occurring). Answer as a fraction
**求抛掷一枚公平硬币四次时至少出现一次正面的概率（正面和反面出现概率相同）。答案以分数形式表示**

**Answer:** 15/16

**Steps:**
- P(at least one head) = 1 - P(no heads)
- P(no heads) = P(TTTT) = (1/2)⁴ = 1/16

**如果事件A和B独立，且P(A)=0.25，P(B)=0.75，求P(A and B)并用你的答案求P(A or B)。只写最终答案，保留3位小数**

## 11. Test the claim that the true mean of TV sets in US homes is equal to 3.
**检验美国家庭电视机真实均值等于3的声称**

**Given:** n = 100, x̄ = 2.84, σ = 0.8, α = 0.02

**Steps:**
- H₀: μ = 3, H₁: μ ≠ 3 (two-tailed test)
- z = (x̄ - μ) ÷ (σ/√n) = (2.84 - 3) ÷ (0.8/√100) = -0.16 ÷ 0.08 = -2.0
- Critical z-value for α = 0.02 (two-tailed): ±2.326
- Since -2.0 > -2.326, do not reject H₀
- Conclusion: No evidence to reject the claim that mean = 3

**知识点:** 双侧z检验 (Two-tailed z-test)

## 12. Test the claim that each student has more than 3 electronic devices.
**检验每个学生拥有超过3个电子设备的声称**

**Given:** σ = 0.8, α = 0.05, n = 100, x̄ = 2.8

**Steps:**
- H₀: μ ≤ 3, H₁: μ > 3 (right-tailed test)
- z = (x̄ - μ) ÷ (σ/√n) = (2.8 - 3) ÷ (0.8/√100) = -0.2 ÷ 0.08 = -2.5
- Critical z-value for α = 0.05 (right-tailed): 1.645
- Since -2.5 < 1.645, do not reject H₀
- Conclusion: No evidence to support the claim that mean > 3

**知识点:** 单侧z检验 (One-tailed z-test)
